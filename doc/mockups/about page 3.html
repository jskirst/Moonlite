Our Philosophy

At MetaBright, we believe that your background doesn't matter. Where you were born, who your family is, what car you drive -- these things don't define a person. All that matters is your knowledge, your abilities, and what you can do. We created MetaBright for people like us. People who believe in the importance of self-development, in mentorship, and in friendly competition.

Use MetaBright to demonstrate what you know. Use it to find out what you don't know. Use it to track your progress through a new skill. Use it to find people who know things you don't know. Use it on your path to mastery. Use it to find out what to learn next. Use it to finally settle debates with your friends. Use it because it's fun and you're bored. Use it because it's funny.
